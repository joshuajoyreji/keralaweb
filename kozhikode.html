About Kozhikode

Kozhikode, historically known as Calicut, is a major city in Kerala, India. Located on the Malabar Coast, Kozhikode is renowned for its rich history, culture, and natural beauty. It was an important trading point for spices and silk in the Middle Ages, attracting traders from across the globe.

Today, Kozhikode is celebrated for its pristine beaches, historic landmarks, vibrant culture, and delectable cuisine, especially its famous Kozhikodan halwa.

  • Kappad Beach - Where Vasco da Gama landed in 1498

  • Thusharagiri Waterfalls - A serene spot amidst lush greenery
  • Mananchira Square - A historic public square with a musical fountain
