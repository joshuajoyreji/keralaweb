Kerala: God's Own Country

Thrissur

Thrissur is known as the cultural capital of Kerala, famous for its Thrissur Pooram festival.
Kozhikode

Kozhikode, historically known as Calicut, is a major city in Kerala famous for its beaches and historical sites.
Ernakulam

Ernakulam is the commercial capital of Kerala, known for its vibrant city life and historical landmarks.
Kollam

Kollam, is a city on the Malabar Coast known for its cashew processing and historical significance.
Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is known for its beautiful beaches and historic landmarks.

Malappuram

Malappuram is known for its rich cultural heritage and educational institutions.

Alappuzha

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is famous for its backwaters, house boats, and coir industry.

Palakkad

Palakkad is known as the gateway to Kerala due to its geographical location and natural beauty.

Kannur

Kannur is known for its pristine beaches, Theyyam rituals, and handloom industry.

Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is known for its religious and cultural significance, often referred to as the 'Pilgrim Capital of Kerala'.

Idukki

Idukki is known for its mountainous terrain, wildlife sanctuaries, and spice plantations.

Wayanad

Wayanad is known for its scenic beauty, wildlife reserves, and trekking trails.

Kasaragod

Kasaragod is known for its unique culture influenced by Karnataka and rich biodiversity.

Kottayam

Kottayam is famous for its rubber plantations and the backwaters of Kumarakom.
